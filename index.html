D Phenomenon

'D Phenomenon', real name 'Divyansh' is a Punjabi music director. He is most known for working with a number of Punjabi singers such as Amrit Gill, The Late Kaka Bhaniawala, Navdeep Wadali, Pratham Verma, Akash Mahal, Nahil Chauhan, Fateh Kahlon and many more. He released his first debut song "TAUR JATT DI" under the record label Xero Creatorz in July 2018 which has smashing "110,330 views" on YouTube. Since releasing his first track in which he featured his own vocals also as a part of Rap Verse in the song, he had been working with a number of singers. He is a music director too, but is also known for his bilingual Rap which features words from English and Punjabi Language.
